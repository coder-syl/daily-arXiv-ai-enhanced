### [5] [Dual-State Slot Attention: Decoupling Appearance and Identity for Video Object-Centric Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12601)
*Sieu Tran,Duc Nguyen,Hao Vo,Khoa Vo,Ngan Le*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文提出Dual-State Slot Attention (DSSA)，通过将外观与身份分离并抑制弱匹配槽的干扰，在无监督视频物体中心学习中显著提升了分割质量与时间一致性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于槽（slot）的方法在快速运动和部分遮挡等复杂场景下难以维持物体身份的稳定性，主要受限于单槽向量同时编码外观与身份导致的目标冲突（即“槽交换”问题），以及Slot Attention中的token重归一化会放大弱响应槽、破坏槽-物体对应关系。

Method: DSSA将每个槽分解为局部状态（用于逐帧外观）和身份状态（用于时序稳定的身份信息）。身份状态通过一个学习到的循环转移机制对局部状态进行时序滤波；同时引入竞争调制聚合（CMA）机制，降低弱匹配槽的更新权重，防止其吸收其他物体的token。

Conclusion: 通过解耦外观与身份表示并抑制弱槽干扰，DSSA有效解决了无监督视频物体中心学习中的槽交换与不稳定性问题，为后续任务提供了更鲁棒的表示。

However, existing slot-based methods struggle to maintain stable object identity in challenging settings such as rapid motion and partial occlusion. First, they typically encode both the per-frame appearance of an object and its identity across frames in a single slot vector, creating an objective conflict that leads to slot swapping: reconstruction requires sensitivity to transient visual changes, whereas temporal consistency requires invariance to them. Second, the token renormalization used in Slot Attention can amplify weakly attending slots, allowing them to absorb tokens from other objects and destabilize slot-to-object correspondence.

Abstract: Vision-language-action (VLA) models generate chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning alongside driving trajectories, but existing benchmarks evaluate only trajectory quality and do not assess whether the CoT is relevant, consistent, or causally connected to the driving action.
